Infinity Stone Collector!

You will be given a random number from Thanos!

There are four infinity stones below. By clicking on a infinity stone, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number Thanos gives you. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each infinity stone is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each infinity stone.
